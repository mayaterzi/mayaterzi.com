Maya Terzi
I’m a creative director and founder working across fashion, branding, and editorial storytelling. My practice sits between design and narrative, building visual worlds that feel both personal and cultural.

I founded Terzi Evi, a brand rooted in heritage craftsmanship and contemporary Mediterranean influence, alongside Cha$e a bag co., a streetwear-driven nonprofit focused on community and visual identity. Additionally, I serve as the Co-founder and Creative Lead for The Walk Thru, an editorial platform exploring the intersection of fashion, sport, and culture.

On track to obtain my B.S. in Creative Industries and Marketing at Bentley University this December 2026, I’m interested in how brands build meaning through imagery, texture, and digital culture and how those worlds translate into physical experience.
